Projects

Single-Image Based Food Volume Estimation

Part of the EU-funded research project Protein. Applying deep learning models to estimate the food volume in a given RGB image. Aiming to establish a user-friendly and scalable system, that trained on easily collected data, is able to accurately estimate the depicted food portions.

Submission to HCI-2020 (extended abstract) Current

Monocular Depth Estimation using Generative Adversarial Networks

A novel approach to monocular depth estimation using Generative Adversarial Networks. The developed model was trained on the KITTI dataset where it exhibited promising results, showing the benefits of GANs versus common generative models.
